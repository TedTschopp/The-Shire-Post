## Suggested sources

- Unsplash: post box, envelope wax seal, country lane, hedgerow, old ledger book, ink pot
- Wikimedia Commons: pillar box, field path, generic postal heritage scenes
- Public domain archives: engraving-style postal marks and letterpress ephemera

## Prohibited image motifs

- Franchise iconography (round green doors, single round windows, dragon imagery, eye motifs)
- Recognizable film-set locations associated with protected franchises
- Stylized fantasy runes or pseudo-Elvish lettering
