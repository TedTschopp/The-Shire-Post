## Suggested sources

- **Unsplash** — search "post box", "envelope wax seal", "country lane", "hedgerow", "old ledger book", "ink pot". Unsplash licence permits commercial use without attribution, but attribution is preferred here.
- **Wikimedia Commons** — search "pillar box", "Royal Mail post box" (historic, generic), "field path". Confirm CC-BY-SA or PD-old for each image.
- **Library of Congress / Public Domain Review** — for engraving-style stamp and postmark plates.

## What to avoid

- Photographs that include franchise iconography (round green doors, single round windows, dragon imagery, eye motifs).
- Images of recognisable Hobbiton film-set locations.
- Stylised "fantasy" calligraphy or runes.
