section i · welcome

The counter is open on Tuesdays and Fridays.

The Shire Post is a quiet correspondence house: a place where letters are franked, parcels are held for collection, and notices are pinned by the door. These public pages are the front of the office.

notice no. 042 · posted this week by the clerk on duty

Held for collection

notice no. 041 · overseas dispatch service update

A pause in foreign parcels

The Post does not at present accept overseas parcels exceeding two pounds in weight. Letters continue to be franked as usual.

notice no. 040 · private correspondence side window

A private door

Beyond this counter lies the working office. Correspondents with standing arrangements may apply at the side window.
